5th Annual Golf Fund Raising Tournament
When: Friday Sept. 27, 2019
Where: Stratton Mountain Golf Club
All proceeds will benefit the Stratton Patrol Fund. More info

The Patrol Fund is a nonprofit and all donations are tax deductible.

The fund supports the local Stratton community along with emergent needs of our patrol family.

In the past, the fund has donated food to local food banks, supported local charities and helped patrollers to further their education in EMS by taking an EMT class.
